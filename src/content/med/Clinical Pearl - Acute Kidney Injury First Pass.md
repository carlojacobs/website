I write AKI as a flowchart in my head: pre-renal, intrinsic, post-renal. The order matters.

## Quick map
1. Check volume status and trends.
2. Look at urine sediment before you order another test.
3. Always remember obstruction.

## Reminder
Urine output is a vital sign. Treat it like one.
